📍 How We Got Here

Every milestone below was built by one 15-year-old, from scratch, with no template.

🗓 January 2026

Platform Goes Live

Neurovascular Dynamic Lab launches publicly on GitHub Pages with full navigation, homepage, and the interactive brain map featuring 12 clickable brain regions.

🗓 February 2026

Firebase Integration Complete

User authentication, cloud-synced quiz scores, and flashcard mastery tracking go live — giving every student a personalised learning dashboard.

🗓 February 2026

Community Launches on Telegram & WhatsApp

The Gideon Reality Telegram channel and WhatsApp group open to students across Nigeria, Ghana, and Kenya. First 100 community members reached within weeks.

🗓 March 2026

Quiz & Flashcard System Released

60 questions across three difficulty tiers and 30 flashcards across 5 neuroscience categories are published — with timed quizzes and cloud score saving.

🗓 April 2026

Resource Library Completed

Nine downloadable resources go live: anatomy posters, a full A–Z neuroscience glossary, worksheets, recommended books, and experiment guides.

🗓 May 2026

200+ Students Reached

The platform crosses 200 students across 8+ countries — all organic, zero advertising. Proof that a good idea, built well, travels on its own.

A Note from the Founder

When I launched this platform, I had no guarantee anyone would use it. I built it because I believed it needed to exist — not because anyone asked me to.

Seeing students from Lagos to Nairobi to Accra actually learning on this platform confirms what I always believed: African students are not behind. We have simply been underserved. That changes now.

Every quiz taken, every flashcard flipped, every PDF downloaded is proof that curiosity has no geography. And we are just getting started.

— Gideon Olukanni, Founder · Neurovascular Dynamic Lab · Age 15 · Nigeria
