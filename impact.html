Our Journey
How We Got Here
47 Days Since Launch

467 Users & Growing

Just 47 days after launch, NVD Lab has reached 467 registered students — and our WhatsApp channel has grown to 220 members.

June 2026 · ~1 Month After Launch

NeuroAI Goes Live, Learn Page Redesigned

NeuroAI — our AI-powered tutor — was integrated across the platform, and the Learn page was fully redesigned for a smoother, more focused study experience.

May 31, 2026 · 6 Days After Launch

237 Active Users in the First Week

Just six days after going live, NVD Lab had already reached 237 active users — a fast, organic start.

May 25, 2026

Neurovascular Dynamic Lab Goes Live

The full platform launched publicly — curriculum, flashcards, and quizzes all available for free from day one.

Early May 2026 · Before Launch

Firebase Integration Built

User accounts, cloud-synced progress, and flashcard/quiz tracking were built in the weeks leading up to launch.

December 2025

WhatsApp Community Channel Launches

Before the platform even existed, our WhatsApp channel was created — the very first place this community began.
